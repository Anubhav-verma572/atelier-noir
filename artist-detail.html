Represented Artist

Elena Vasquez

Medium / Focus Abstract Expressionism, Oil on Canvas, Mixed Media

Born in Madrid and currently working from her studio in Brooklyn, Elena Vasquez creates large-scale abstract works that explore the emotional resonance of color and texture. Her method is deeply physical, often applying heavy impasto layers of oil paint mixed with raw pigments, ash, and gold leaf.

Her work has been featured in major contemporary exhibitions across Europe and North America, drawing critical acclaim for its striking balance between aggressive structural forms and delicate luminosity.
