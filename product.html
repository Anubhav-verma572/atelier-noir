Vasquez's "Nocturne in Gold" explores the tension between deep void spaces and aggressive textural interventions. Using layered oils mixed with raw pigment and meticulously applied 24K gold leaf, the canvas catches light differently depending on the time of day, creating a living, breathing presence within a room.

This piece is part of her highly acclaimed "Midnight Meridian" series, exhibited in Barcelona in late 2023.
